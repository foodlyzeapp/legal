2. Description of Service

Foodlyze is a calorie tracking application that provides:

  • AI Food Analysis: Analyzes food photos to estimate nutritional content
  • Voice Input: Hands-free food entry using speech recognition
  • Barcode Scanning: Scans product barcodes to retrieve nutrition data
  • Calorie Tracking: Tracks daily calorie and macronutrient intake
  • AI Coach: Provides personalized dietary insights (Premium)
  • Statistics: Displays weekly and monthly nutrition trends
  • PDF Reports: Export nutrition reports (Premium)
  • HealthKit Integration: Syncs with Apple Health (optional)
  • Multi-language Support: Available in 29 languages
