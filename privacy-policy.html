Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

Welcome to Foodlyze, developed by Harun Can Ayaz. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Profile Information

  • Date of birth, gender, height, and weight
  • Activity level and fitness goals
  • Daily calorie and macro targets

Health & Nutrition Data

  • Food entries with nutritional information
  • Calorie, protein, carbohydrate, and fat intake
  • Water consumption tracking
  • Meal categorization and history
  • Favorite foods and frequently consumed items

Photos & Media

  • Food photos captured via camera or selected from gallery
  • Photos are sent to AI services for nutritional analysis
  • Photos are stored locally on your device

Voice Data

  • Voice recordings for hands-free food entry
  • Audio is processed entirely on-device using Apple Speech Recognition
  • Voice recordings are never stored or transmitted to external servers
  • Only converted text is sent for food analysis

Barcode Data

  • Product barcodes scanned for food identification
  • Barcode data is used to retrieve nutritional information from databases

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Food Analysis: Photos and text are processed by AI to identify foods and estimate nutritional values
  • Barcode Lookup: Scanned barcodes retrieve product nutrition data
  • AI Coach: Your nutrition data may be analyzed to provide personalized dietary insights
  • Calorie Tracking: To track and display your daily nutritional intake
  • Personalization: To calculate personalized daily goals based on your profile
  • Health Integration: To sync data with Apple HealthKit with your permission
  • Localization: To display the app in your preferred language

3. Data Storage

Local-First Privacy: All your personal data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal information. Your data stays on your device and under your control.

  • Profile and nutrition data is stored using Core Data on your device
  • Food photos are stored locally on your device
  • Voice recordings are processed in real-time and never stored
  • Favorite foods and history are stored locally
  • Data is not uploaded to our servers

4. Premium Subscription

Foodlyze offers optional premium subscriptions with enhanced features:

  • Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store
  • Payment information is handled entirely by Apple
  • We never receive or store your payment details
  • Subscription status is stored locally on your device

5. Third-Party Services

Foodlyze integrates with the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: Food photos and text are processed for AI-powered nutritional analysis. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Apple Speech Recognition: Voice input is processed on-device
  • Apple HealthKit: Optional sync of nutrition data with Apple Health
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Anonymous crash reports to improve app stability
  • Open Food Facts: Barcode database for product information

6. Permissions We Request

  • Camera: Take food photos and scan barcodes
  • Photo Library: Select existing food photos
  • Microphone: Voice food entry feature
  • Speech Recognition: Convert voice to text on-device
  • HealthKit: Sync with Apple Health (optional)
  • Notifications: Reminders (optional)

7. Data Retention

  • Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
  • Voice recordings are processed in real-time and never stored
  • You can delete all data anytime from Settings
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

8. Children's Privacy

Foodlyze is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data stored locally on your device
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of HealthKit integration
  • Deny any permissions
  • Cancel your premium subscription anytime

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
