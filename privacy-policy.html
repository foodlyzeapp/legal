Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 24, 2026

Welcome to Foodlyze, developed by Harun Can Ayaz. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

App Privacy Summary

Data Linked to You

  • Contact Info: Name (for personalization)
  • Health & Fitness: HealthKit data (steps, calories burned)
  • Purchases: Premium subscription status

Data Not Linked to You

  • User Content: Photos (food analysis), Audio (voice input)
  • Identifiers: Device ID (Firebase)
  • Usage Data: Product interaction (Firebase Analytics)
  • Diagnostics: Crash data, Performance data (Firebase Crashlytics)

1. Information We Collect

Contact Information

  • Name (used for personalized greetings like "Good morning, [Name]")

Profile Information

  • Date of birth, gender, height, and weight
  • Activity level and fitness goals
  • Daily calorie and macro targets

Health & Fitness Data

  • Food entries with nutritional information
  • Calorie, protein, carbohydrate, and fat intake
  • Water consumption tracking
  • Meal categorization and history
  • Favorite foods and frequently consumed items
  • Apple HealthKit data: steps, distance, active minutes, calories burned (with your permission)

Photos & Media

  • Food photos captured via camera or selected from gallery
  • Photos are sent to our secure server for AI-powered nutritional analysis
  • Photos are processed and not permanently stored on our servers
  • Photos are stored locally on your device

Voice Data

  • Voice recordings for hands-free food entry
  • Audio is processed on-device using Apple Speech Recognition
  • Voice recordings are never stored or transmitted to external servers
  • Only converted text is sent for food analysis

Barcode Data

  • Product barcodes scanned for food identification
  • Barcode data is used to retrieve nutritional information from Open Food Facts database

Usage Data

  • App usage patterns via Firebase Analytics
  • Feature interactions and screen views
  • This data is collected anonymously and not linked to your identity

Diagnostic Data

  • Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Performance metrics (app launch time, response times)
  • This data helps us improve app stability and performance
  • Diagnostic data is not linked to your identity

Identifiers

  • Device ID for Firebase services
  • Used for analytics and crash reporting
  • Not linked to your personal identity

Purchase Information

  • Premium subscription status
  • Subscription is processed through Apple's App Store
  • We never receive or store your payment details

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalization: Name is used for personalized greetings and AI Coach messages
  • Food Analysis: Photos and text are processed by AI to identify foods and estimate nutritional values
  • Barcode Lookup: Scanned barcodes retrieve product nutrition data
  • AI Coach: Your nutrition data is analyzed to provide personalized dietary insights (Premium)
  • Calorie Tracking: To track and display your daily nutritional intake
  • Health Goals: To calculate personalized daily goals based on your profile
  • Health Integration: To sync data with Apple HealthKit with your permission
  • App Improvement: Analytics and crash data help us improve the app
  • Localization: To display the app in your preferred language

3. Data Security

Security Measures: We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data during transmission and processing.

Security Features

  • Secure API Communication: All data transmitted to our servers uses Firebase Cloud Functions with HTTPS encryption
  • Rate Limiting: Protection against abuse with request limits
  • Input Validation: All inputs are validated and sanitized
  • Image Size Limits: Maximum 5MB per image for security
  • Language Validation: Only supported languages are processed
  • Response Sanitization: All API responses are sanitized before being returned
  • Firebase App Check: Validates that requests come from our genuine app
  • No Direct API Key Exposure: API keys are stored securely on our servers, never in the app

4. Data Storage

Local-First Privacy: Your personal profile and nutrition data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal information on our servers.

  • Profile and nutrition data is stored using Core Data on your device
  • Food photos are stored locally on your device
  • Voice recordings are processed in real-time and never stored
  • Favorite foods and history are stored locally
  • Analytics data is stored anonymously by Firebase
  • Crash reports are stored anonymously by Firebase Crashlytics

6. Permissions We Request

  • Camera: Take food photos and scan barcodes
  • Photo Library: Select existing food photos
  • Microphone: Voice food entry feature
  • Speech Recognition: Convert voice to text on-device
  • HealthKit: Sync with Apple Health (optional)
  • Notifications: Smart reminders (optional)

7. Data Retention

  • Your local data remains on your device until you choose to delete it
  • Voice recordings are processed in real-time and never stored
  • Food photos sent for analysis are not permanently stored on our servers
  • Firebase Analytics data is retained according to Firebase's retention policies
  • You can delete all local data anytime from Settings
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

8. Children's Privacy

Foodlyze is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data stored locally on your device
  • Delete your data at any time via Settings
  • Export your data (PDF reports)
  • Opt-out of HealthKit integration
  • Deny any permissions
  • Cancel your premium subscription anytime
  • Request information about data we process

10. Data Tracking

We do NOT use your data for tracking purposes such as:

  • Targeted advertising
  • Sharing with data brokers
  • Cross-app tracking
  • Third-party advertising

Your data is used solely for app functionality, personalization, and improving the app experience.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
