Welcome to Foodlyze ("the App"), developed by Harun Can Ayaz ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Foodlyze collects the following types of information:

a) Profile Information

  • Gender, age, height, and weight
  • Activity level and fitness goals
  • This information is used to calculate your personalized daily calorie and macro targets

b) Health & Nutrition Data

  • Food entries and calorie intake
  • Water consumption
  • Macronutrient data (protein, carbohydrates, fat)

c) Photos

  • Food photos taken with camera or selected from gallery
  • Photos are sent to OpenAI's API for AI-powered food analysis
  • Photos are stored locally on your device

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Food Analysis: Photos are sent to OpenAI's API to identify food items and estimate nutritional values
  • Calorie Tracking: To track your daily calorie and nutrient intake
  • Personalization: To calculate personalized daily goals based on your profile
  • Health Integration: To sync data with Apple HealthKit (with your permission)

3. Data Storage

âš¡ Important: All your personal data (profile, food entries, photos) is stored locally on your device only. We do not have servers that store your personal information.
  • Profile and nutrition data is stored using Core Data on your device
  • Photos are stored locally on your device
  • Data is not uploaded to our servers
  • Data is not shared with third parties except as described below

4. Third-Party Services

Foodlyze uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: Food photos are sent to OpenAI for AI-powered nutritional analysis. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy
  • Apple HealthKit: With your permission, we may read/write health data to Apple Health
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Anonymous crash reports to improve app stability

5. Permissions We Request

  • Camera: To take photos of food for analysis
  • Photo Library: To select existing food photos for analysis
  • HealthKit: To sync calorie and nutrition data with Apple Health (optional)
  • Notifications: To send water intake reminders (optional)

6. Data Retention

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete it
  • You can delete all data at any time from Settings > Delete All Data
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

7. Children's Privacy

Foodlyze is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data at any time
  • Opt-out of HealthKit integration
  • Disable notifications

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
